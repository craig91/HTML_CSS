For a one-year period from November 2010, the Marugame Genchiro-Inokuma Museum of Contemporary Art (MIMOCA) will host a cycle of four Hiroshi Sugimoto exhibitions.

Each will showcase works by the artist thematically contextualized under the headings "Science," "Architecture," "History," and "Religion," so as to present a comprehensive panorama if the artist's oevure.

Hours 10:00 - 18:00 (No admittance after 17:30)
